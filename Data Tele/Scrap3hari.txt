Weekly Rules :
Price about to break with high vol or above MA10 weekly for at least 3 weeks. Better IF Daily chart form higher low
Price on move closer toward MA50 weekly with preferebly steady increase or tight price consolidation
MA10 weekly and MA50 weekly narrowing and ready to cross up
IF price pullback,volume dry up when price consolidate around MA10 weekly with tight base price
IF Price break up MA10 weekly, MA10 cross MA50 weekly indicating a very strong trend
Strong trend usually caused by earning surprise, Corp act,Sectoral cycle or overall market condition
LEVEL TO WATCH : MA10W = MA50D  MA50W=MA200D

Daily Rules :
Look at the movement of daily price has break MA10 daily for at least 1 Week with high volume
Watch for the next day movement, if price can hold up wait until the MA10 follow the price
Price form higher low, down between 10-20% is the best, or max 33% with price hold up above MA10 Daily
IF Buying in pullback, meaning wait the price to build a base near MA10 creating some "U" shape or just tight price movement at least 1 week 
If the price crossing down MA10 but still above MA50 with MA10 narrowing the MA50 when making a pullback base and the price hold up at least 1 week, see the opportunity to entry.
For very Strong uptrend, MA10 daily must above MA50 and MA200 daily for at least 1 Week


When to be patient and Hold or Add more the Stock :
IF buying new position, stay in phase with general market, tighten stop loss (3-5%) needed if the general market is on Bear condition
IF stock up 15% from buy point we can move SL, stock has up 20% must stay profit when sell it
The rules to avg up is add more when stock advance 2-3% from initial buy or wait for the price pullback to MA10 daily in searching for entry reason. Chasing the strong movement = higher risk
KEEP YOUR PORTFOLIO CLEAN AND IN SYNCHRONY WITH THE MARKET
Quality Stock that has up 20% in less than 8 weeks should be held until selling climax. In many case stock that up more than 20% in 1-4 week with increasing vol are the most powerfull candidat. 
Correction or pullback usually 10-20% or still above MA10 Weekly

 When you buy or sell a stock and it does not act right immediately or start to move in your favor within a reasonable length of time, get out of it. Your judgment gets worse the longer you hold on and hope for the market to go your way, and at extremes you always do the wrong thing. It is much better to take a quick loss of 2, 3, or 5  points than to hold on and hope and eventually take anywhere from a 10 to a 50-point loss. 
 If stocks won t go your way, you must go their way.

Always go with the tide; never buck it. 

If you were on a railroad track and saw a train coming at 60 miles an hour, would you stand there and hope that the train would stop before it hit you, or would you hope that maybe you could knock it off the track? Of course you wouldn t. You would get out of the way and do it quick. 

You should do the same thing in the stock market -- Get out; let them go by, or get aboard and ride with them.

How Stocks are Sold

 A good business man advertises his goods and that is what the manipulators do. When they wish to distribute stocks and get them into the hands of the public, they use the newspapers in every way possible to advertise the stock. Their fluctuations are given wide publicity and everything possible is done to attract the public.
It requires wide fluctuations and activity to entice the public to take a hand. 

They may pay very little attention
to a stock selling around 40 when it is only fluctuating 5 or 6 points in three or four months, but when this same stock reaches 150 and begins to fluctuate 5 and 10 points each day, everybody talks about it. They see great opportunities for making big profits and begin to trade in it. The result is that the wide publicity and advertising induces the public to buy all the stock at a high price. Then the decline starts. They hold on and hope, and nothing much is said about it until the stock gets near the bottom, when all the bad news comes out and everybody talks about it.
